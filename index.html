Play

The cards are auto-arranged in 8 slots randomly at the start of the game. You will be given 10 seconds to remember the positions of each card, and then the cards will move away to a container besides.

Now, you are to arrange the cards in their respective slots. The game is of 80 points, 10 points each correct position you define.

- There is no limit in arranging of card.
- You can place a card once only at a place.
- (Imp) Dont overlap over cards, if once placed, or else you lose one card for nothing.

Hit submit once you finish placing all the cards..

That's it. A piece of cake, and you are set to go.
Instructions: As you press, Let's Go !!! you will directed to game, where as you press Start, your ten seconds countdown starts on for remembering the positions.
